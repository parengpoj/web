Pojol Audit and Tax Consultancy Services in 2018, Pojol and Gabay, Certified Public Accountants is a full service accounting, audit and tax firm located in Caloocan City (North), Metro Manila, Philippines, and is a registered general professional partnership of Filipino Certified Public Accountants, duly accredited by the Professional Regulatory Board of Accountancy.

About the Firm

Our Mission

To provide professional services and educate clients about proper financial reporting and tax compliance.

Our Vision

To be an advocate of best practices in accounting, financial reporting and proper tax compliance.

Our Core Values

• Honesty • Integrity • Trust

Our Services

Audit and Assurance

We provide independent and professional opinions that reduce information risk (risk from incorrect information).

Financial Statements Preparation

We assist in the preparation of financial statements based from related financial transactions and information.

Tax Compliance

We assist in the filing of various tax returns and related compliance reports. We also assist in tax audit, serve as an independent CPA before the Court of Tax Appeals.

Trainings, Seminars and Webinars

We provide trainings, seminars and webinars on related topics on accounting and Philippine taxation.

Business Registration and Amendments

We assist in registration and amendments of business registration information with various government agencies.

Trademark Registration

We assist in the registration of trademarks before the Intellectual Property Office of the Philippines.

Corporate Secretarial Services

We provide services as an appointed corporate secretary and assist in the compliance of various corporate requirements before the Philippine Securities and Exchange Commission.

Other Agreed-Upon Engagements

We provide other agreed upon engagements that require the service or assistance of an independent CPA.
