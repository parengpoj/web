Pojol Audit and Tax Consultancy Services in 2018, Pojol and Gabay, Certified Public Accountants is a full service accounting, audit and tax firm located in Caloocan City (North), in the northernmost area of Metro Manila (National Capital Region), in the Philippines, and is a duly registered general professional partnership of Filipino Certified Public Accountants.

About Us
Founded by Mr. Mark Anthony Pojol as M. Pojol Audit and Tax Consultancy Services, a sole proprietorship in 2018, the firm has converted into a general professional partnership with the admission of Mr. Ricky Gabay as a partner in 2021, to serve its clients better. The firm is a full service accounting, audit and tax firm located in Caloocan City (North), in the northernmost area of Metro Manila (National Capital Region), in the Philippines.

The Partners

Mark Anthony Pojol
Managing Partner

Mark, also called Poj by his colleagues, leads the firm as its managing partner. He also handle the external audit engagements as an accredited CPA in public practice. 

He passed the CPA Licensure Examinations in 2008 and obtained his B.S. in Accountancy degree from the Polytechnic University of the Philippines (Manila) in 2007.

He also has a Post Baccalaureate Diploma in Information Technology from the Polytechnic University of the Philippines - Open University in 2018.

Ricky Gabay
Partner

Ricky is a partner of the firm. He also serves as the municipal accountant in the town of Lapinig in Northern Samar. 

He passed the CPA Licensure Examinations in 2011 and obtained his B.S. in Accountancy degree from the University of Eastern Philippines (Catarman, Northern Samar) in 2010.
