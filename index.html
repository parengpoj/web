Professional Guidance is our Personal Guarantee

Founded as M. Pojol Audit and Tax Consultancy Services in 2018, Pojol and Gabay, Certified Public Accountants is a full service accounting, audit and tax firm located in Caloocan City (North), in the northernmost area of Metro Manila (National Capital Region), in the Philippines, and is a duly registered general professional partnership of Filipino Certified Public Accountants duly accredited by the Professional Regulatory Board of Accountancy (Philippines).

We are an independent member of OneSMP, a network of business consultants and professional firms in the ASEAN region, and Moores Rowland Philippines, network of Filipino accounting professionals and consultants. We are also a partner of JuanTax, the Philippine's first online tax platform solution.

About Us
Founded by Mr. Mark Anthony Pojol as M. Pojol Audit and Tax Consultancy Services, a sole proprietorship in 2018, the firm has converted into a general professional partnership with the admission of Mr. Ricky Gabay as a partner in 2021, and Mr. Daniel Guevarra in 2023, to serve its clients better. The firm is a full service accounting, audit and tax firm duly accredited with the Professional Regulatory Board of Accountancy (Philippines). Our main office is located in Caloocan City (North), in the northernmost area of Metro Manila (National Capital Region), Philippines.

The Partners

Mark Anthony V. Pojol
Managing Partner

Mark, also called Poj by his colleagues, leads the firm as its managing partner. He also leads the external audit engagements of the firm. 

He passed the CPA Licensure Examinations in October 2008 and obtained his B.S. in Accountancy degree from the Polytechnic University of the Philippines (Manila) in 2007. He also has a Post Baccalaureate Diploma in Information Technology from the PUP - Open University in 2018.

He is also a member of the Philippine Institute of Certified Public Accountants (PICPA), Association of CPAs in Public Practice (ACPAPP) and the Philippine Institute of Cybersecurity Professionals (PICSPro).

Ricky L. Gabay
Partner

Ricky is a partner and concurrently serving as the municipal accountant of Lapinig, Northern Samar. 

He passed the CPA Licensure Examinations in May 2011 and obtained his B.S. in Accountancy degree from the University of Eastern Philippines (Catarman, Northern Samar) in 2010.

He is also a member of the Philippine Institute of Certified Public Accountants (PICPA), Association of CPAs in Public Practice (ACPAPP) and the Philippine Association of Local Government Accountants (PhALGA).

Daniel B. Guevarra
Associate Partner

Daniel is the associate partner and assists the managing partner in the day-to-day activities of the firm and acts as the overall supervisor of the accounting engagements.

He passed the CPA Licensure Examinations in May 2017 and obtained his B.S. in Management Accounting degree from University of Sto. Tomas in 2015, and B.S. in Accountancy degree from Colegio de San Juan de Letran in 2016.

He is also a member of the Philippine Institute of Certified Public Accountants (PICPA), Association of CPAs in Public Practice (ACPAPP) and the Institute of Certified Bookkeepers (MICB).
